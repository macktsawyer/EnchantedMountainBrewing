About Us


Located in the beautiful foothills of the Enchanted Mountain range of Western New York; the Enchanted Mountain Brewing Company has created a wide range of delicious beverages for the past five years.
EMBC offers a wide range of different specialties and seasonal options. You are sure to find something to soothe your palette. With a delicious assortment of meals you are destined to leave satisfied.

Our Motto

Enchanted Mountain Brewing Co. was built with the customer's satisfaction in mind. Other breweries like to brew for themselves and sell their craft on the side. We brew with the customer in mind and always want you to leave happier than when you arrived.
EMBC is constantly looking to perfect our trade and make the experience of drinking and dining with us better.

Our Beer Selection

Maple Brandy Pilsner
A sweet and aromatic pilsner with a slight hint of maple. Best enjoyed on warm days in the fall.

Amber Tree Top Lager
A favorite among our locals the Amber Tree Top Lager is staple in our selection. One of our first brews we best enjoy this poured in a hearty glass with a nice top.

Rock City Slate Milk Stout
A dark and creamy stout with a subtle taste of chocolate and dark coffee smell.

State & Union Stout
Another beautifully dark brew with a rich flavor and aromatic head.

Home Town Pale Ale
A light and refreshing pale ale with hoppy and flowery aromas and sweet bitter taste. Smooth and crisp taste.

Oak Hill Porter
A dark and hearty porter with flavorful after taste that resembles apples and cream.
